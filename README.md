# [Game "Rock Paper Scissors"](https://user-crud-yuskiv.netlify.app/)

## Let’s implement the famous kid game! 
Everybody knows the rules:
-	Scissors beats a paper,
-	Paper beats rock,
-	Rock beats scissors.
-	And we play up to three wins!

## Just for fun:

Choose the complexity of the game (number of items).

Make your move (use the buttons on the left side of the window or click on the icon of the corresponding object).

Good luck.
